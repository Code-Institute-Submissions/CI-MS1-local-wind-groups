Purpose

The aim of this directory is to grow community involvement and spread awareness of brass bands, concert bands, orchestras and music services that are available in South East London.

The directory is envisaged to be of use for woodwind and brass players seeking out new groups to play in, as well as organisations looking to engage groups for participation in events.
